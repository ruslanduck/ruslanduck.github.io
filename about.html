Our Story

We've been on
both sides
of the build.

Duck Agency was born from operational pain. Not from a developer's desk, but from the trenches of running a growing team with broken tools.

From 80 people
in spreadsheets
to building the
systems that
replace them.

Before Duck Agency, Ruslan ran an 80-person customer care department at a fast-growing e-commerce startup in Ukraine. Five employees grew to 80 in 18 months. KPIs, scheduling, quality assurance, reporting — everything manual.

The development team couldn't help for a year. So Ruslan built the automation himself using Airtable. Within months, the management team had 50% more time for strategic decisions instead of firefighting.

That's when the realization hit: he understood operations problems because he'd lived them. Not studied them from a developer's chair. Duck Agency was built on that dual perspective.

2018 – 2021

Operations Leader

Running 80-person department at a fast-growing e-commerce startup. Facing the same chaos clients face today. Building the first automations out of necessity.

2021 – 2023

First Clients

Companies started asking Ruslan to build the same systems for them. First client was a hostel chain and recruitment company in Czech Republic who asked him to relocate.

2024

Duck Agency Founded

Formalized the agency. Focused exclusively on operational systems and automation. Stopped taking website projects. The focus changed everything.

2024 – Now

30+ Systems Delivered

Projects across manufacturing, engineering, e-commerce, logistics, staffing, hospitality, and corporate travel. Clients in US, Czech Republic, Ukraine, Germany, and across Europe.
